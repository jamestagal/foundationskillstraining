Why choose Foundation Skills Training

We provide a range of Foundation Skills specific services. See more below!

Holistic Approach

We take a holistic approach to education and professional development. We promote foundation skills are not just about literacy and numeracy, digital and employability skills but also that they are essential to being successful functioning individual..

Customisation

Our services and courses are tailored to the specific needs of individuals and organizations. We don't believe in one-size-fits-all solutions and our training will reflect your needs and requirements.

Interactive Learning

Our interactive e-learning modules make learning engaging and effective, ensuring that learners stay motivated and on track. We put the learning experience first.

Growth mindset

We actively combat learned helplessness by instilling a growth mindset in our learners. We believe that with the right support and resources, anyone can improve their foundation skills.

Data-Driven Results

We believe in measuring progress and impact. Our data-driven insights and teching methods ensure that our clients can see tangible improvements in foundation skills.

Advocacy for Change

We're not just a business; we're advocates for change. We work to change the narrative around foundation skills, emphasizing their crucial role in lifelong learning and career success.


- Consulting Services -

Foundation Skills Training's consulting services provide customised learning and assessments, professional development, and curriculum integration tailored to address individual and organizational needs, ultimately enhancing foundation skills, promoting growth mindsets, and driving success in education and the workplace.

01.

Customised Foundation Skills Learning & Assessments:

We provide comprehensive assessments to identify an individual's specific strengths and weaknesses in areas such as English language, literacy, numeracy, and digital skills (LLND). Our assessments are designed to help individuals, educational institutions, and businesses gain insights into areas that need improvement.

02.

Strategic Professional Development:

We collaborate with organizations to create tailored strategies for enhancing foundation skills among their employees. Our team of experts helps businesses identify areas where improved foundation skills can boost productivity and employee engagement.

03.

Curriculum Development and Integration:

We assist educational institutions in integrating foundation skills into their curricula, ensuring students are equipped with essential skills for career success.
